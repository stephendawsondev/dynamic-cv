Nobody's got time for that!

The job market is tough! A generic CV doesn't cut it anymore. For each job post, you should be tailoring your CV - you should have the correct title, you should include the relevant skills, work experience and education. If you're going for a role as a Backend Developer, they won't be interested in your fancy CSS skills.


But who has the time to do that? You're probably applying to multiple jobs a day. You need a tool that can help you create a tailored CV in minutes, not hours.


That's where Dynamic CV comes in. Dynamic CV allows you to create a CV that is tailored to the job you're applying for. You can create multiple CVs and save them for later. You can also download them as a PDF or even a word document.

{% include 'includes/question-mark.svg' %}

How does it work?

Here's how Dynamic CV works: You start by putting all your professional info into the system - your jobs, education, skills, projects, and anything else that might not fit on a typical CV. When you're ready to apply for a job, just paste in the job posting.


Our smart system analyses the job post and picks out the most important skills they're after. Then comes the fun part - you get to cherry-pick from your own experience, matching what you've got to what they want. It's building a custom CV, but without the headache.


In just a few clicks,you've got a CV that's tailor-made for that specific job. No more one-size-fits-all applications - just you, showing off exactly why you're perfect for the role..
