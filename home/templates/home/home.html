Nobody's got time for that!

The job market is tough! A generic CV doesn't cut it anymore. For each job post, you should be tailoring your CV - you should have the correct title, you should include the relevant skills, work experience and education. If you're going for a role as a Backend Developer, they won't be interested in your fancy CSS skills.


But who has the time to do that? You're probably applying to multiple jobs a day. You need a tool that can help you create a tailored CV in minutes, not hours.


That's where Dynamic CV comes in. Dynamic CV allows you to create a CV that is tailored to the job you're applying for. You can create multiple CVs and save them for later. You can also download them as a PDF or even a word document.

How does it work?

Here's how Dynamic CV functions: Begin by entering all your professional information into the system—such as your work experience, education, skills, projects, and any other details that might not typically fit on a standard CV. When you're interested in a specific job, simply add the position title and run our analysis tool.


Our intelligent CV analysis tool evaluates the job title on your CV against the top skills typically required for that role in the market. It provides personalized feedback, highlighting key skills for the position, how well your CV aligns with these requirements, and checking for any spelling errors. Additionally, it reviews your previous job titles to identify relevant experience that might cover any skills missing from your CV compared to the top industry standards.


With just a few clicks, you'll have a CV perfectly tailored to the specific job you want. Say goodbye to generic, one-size-fits-all applications, and showcase exactly why you're the ideal candidate for the role.
